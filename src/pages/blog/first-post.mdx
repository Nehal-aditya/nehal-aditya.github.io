This is the body of my very first blog post. I'm excited to share my thoughts here!

Welcome to this space where I'll be documenting my journey through technology, science, and software development. As someone passionate about web development and curious about science concepts, I'm always learning new things — and this blog is where I'll share those discoveries.

## What to Expect

This blog will cover a range of topics:

- **Web Development** — From modern frameworks like Astro, React, and Next.js to the fundamentals of building great user experiences.
- **Science & Technology** — Explorations of scientific concepts and how they intersect with computing.
- **AI & Machine Learning** — As part of the Deepcomet AI ecosystem, I'll share insights on AI-native computing, the Aurelia language, and more.
- **Project Updates** — Progress reports on the Deepcomet AI ecosystem, GeminiChat, and other projects.

## The Journey So Far

Building software is not just about writing code — it's about understanding problems, designing solutions, and iterating until you've created something that truly helps people.

My journey started with curiosity about how things work — from web browsers to operating systems, from neural networks to compiler design. That curiosity eventually led me to **Deepcomet AI**, where we're building an entire computing stack designed for AI-native workloads.

I believe in the power of open source, collaborative development, and sharing knowledge. Every project I work on is an opportunity to learn something new and contribute back to the community. Whether it's a web app built with Astro or a systems language compiler targeting NPUs, the principles are the same: solve real problems, share your work, and keep learning.
